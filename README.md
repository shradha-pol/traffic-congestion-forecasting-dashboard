# 🚦 Traffic Congestion Forecasting Dashboard

An end-to-end Data Analytics project that analyzes traffic congestion patterns using sensor-generated traffic data and visualizes actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## 🧾 Project Overview

Traffic congestion is one of the major challenges faced by modern cities. Increasing vehicle density, peak-hour traffic, and weather conditions significantly impact traffic flow and road efficiency.

This project leverages traffic sensor data to analyze congestion trends, identify high-risk road segments, evaluate weather impacts, and provide decision-support insights through interactive dashboards.

The project follows a complete analytics pipeline from data preparation to visualization using Excel, MySQL, Python, Data Science techniques, and Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze traffic volume and congestion patterns.
* Identify peak and off-peak traffic periods.
* Detect congestion hotspots across locations and road segments.
* Evaluate the impact of weather conditions on traffic flow.
* Compare traffic behavior across vehicle categories.
* Monitor traffic performance using interactive dashboards.
* Generate actionable insights for traffic management.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Fog conditions create the highest congestion levels.
* Peak-hour traffic significantly reduces average vehicle speed.
* Silk Board Junction is the most congested road segment.
* Weekday traffic volume is higher than weekend traffic volume.
* Cars contribute the largest share of total traffic.
* Intersections experience higher congestion severity than highways.
